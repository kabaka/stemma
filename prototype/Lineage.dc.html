Reading your own records into Lineage is feasible through patient-mediated access (SMART on FHIR, Apple Health, aggregators like 1upHealth) — a provider connect flow would land your conditions, medications and labs here automatically. Writing data back to a provider is the hard part: EHRs almost never accept external records as authoritative, so the realistic path is exporting a document above for a clinician to review and reconcile.
