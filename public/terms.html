In an emergency, call 911 or your local emergency number.

What Stemma does

Stemma helps you build a family health history and pedigree and surface hereditary-risk patterns worth raising with a clinician. With your explicit authorization, it can read a copy of your own health record from a provider you choose (via the SMART on FHIR standard). This access is read-only — Stemma never writes to, changes, or deletes anything in your provider's record — and you can disconnect at any time, which removes the stored connection and any tokens from your browser.
